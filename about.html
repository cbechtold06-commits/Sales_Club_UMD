Faculty Advisor

Jess Gardner

Jess is an Instructor of Marketing and the Director of the Professional Sales Program at the University of Minnesota Duluth. She has over 20 years of experience in the sales industry and higher education. Prior to academia, she was selling pharmaceutical products for a division of Johnson & Johnson. As an instructor at both UW-Stout and UW-Eau Claire, she has taught various marketing courses, including Professional Selling, Advanced Sales, Sales Management & Sales Internship. Jessica continues to have a focus on student success beyond the classroom setting, some of this can be seen in her role as faculty advisor for the UMD Sale Club. She has a MBA from the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire and a BBA in marketing from Wisconsin School of Business
